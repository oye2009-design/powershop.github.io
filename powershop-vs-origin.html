3. Solar Strategy: Optimization vs. Export

There is a nuance here often missed in basic reviews.

  • Choose Powershop if: You have a battery or EV. Their app provides granular data (down to the half-hour) helping you shift usage to solar hours. Optimizing self-consumption saves you ~30c/kWh (buying from grid).
